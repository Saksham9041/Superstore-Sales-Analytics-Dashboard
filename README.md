📊 Super Store Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

🚀 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Sales Analytics Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze sales performance, profit trends, and customer behavior across different regions and categories. The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by providing clear and actionable business insights.


🎯 Business Objective

* Analyze sales and profit performance across regions and segments
* Identify high-performing categories and sub-categories
* Track monthly trends and year-over-year growth
* Understand customer purchasing behavior and shipping preferences


🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
* SQL (Data extraction & transformation)
* Python (Data cleaning & preprocessing)
* Excel (Initial data handling)


📊 Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards: **Total Sales, Profit, Average Delivery Time**
* Sales Analysis by **Segment, Category, and Sub-Category**
* Region-wise performance analysis (Central, East, South, West)
* Sales by Payment Mode and Shipping Mode
* Monthly Sales & Profit trends (Year-over-Year comparison)
* Interactive filters and slicers for dynamic insights


📈 Key Insights

* Consumer segment contributes the highest share of total sales
* Standard shipping mode dominates overall deliveries
* Significant variation in monthly profit trends across years
* Certain sub-categories (e.g., Phones, Chairs) drive major revenue
* Regional analysis highlights performance differences across zones
